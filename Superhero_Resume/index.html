TONY STARK

Genius, Billionaire, Playboy, Philanthropist!


   SUMMARY

Tony Stark, with his unparalleled mind and revolutionary technology, has left an indelible mark on the world. This resume showcases his academic brilliance, pioneering projects, pivotal experiences, and world-saving achievements.

Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

  • Bachelor of Science in Physics and Electrical Engineering (Graduated at age 17)
  • Independent Research - Advanced studies in theoretical physics, robotics, and artificial intelligence

   PROJECTS

  • Iron Man Armor Series:
    Development and continuous improvement of a series of technologically advanced exoskeletons, culminating in the Mark LXXXV suit, granting superhuman strength, flight, and repulsor technology.

  • Stark Industries Arc Reactor:
    Miniaturized clean energy technology, providing limitless energy with zero emissions.

  • Jarvis Artificial Intelligence:
    Creation of a self-evolving AI assistant capable of managing complex systems, strategic planning, and even humor.
